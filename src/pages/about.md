I am Zheng Shi. Computer scientist and machine learning engineer.

I have spent 12 years building ML/optimization systems in both enterprise and startup environments. At IBM, I grew from entry level data scientist to ML technical lead and Team Lead, worked on problems across supply chain optimization, recommendation, ranking, and forecasting. At Oden Technologies, I joined as their first Staff ML engineer and built the entire recsys and anomaly detection system from scratch. In parallel with all of that, I completed a PhD in machine learning and optimization at Lehigh University while working full-time.

I am currently on sabbatical, spending my time thinking, writing, building, and enjoying life.
